// My Story

How it all started.

From an early fascination with numbers to a career dedicated to uncovering insights — I've worked on dashboards that support HR planning, analysed workforce and engagement trends, and presented data-driven insights to university leadership.

After earning a Bachelor's degree in Finance, I discovered my passion for analytics and decided to pursue it wholeheartedly. Today, I'm deepening my expertise through an MBA in Business Analytics at Saint Peter's University (GPA 3.8/4.0).

I previously completed coursework in Human Resource Management at Thompson Rivers University before transferring, inspired by hands-on analytical work during my HR Analytics Co-op at The Home Depot. I enjoy blending business context with analytical rigour to turn raw data into clear insights.

If you're looking for someone who can combine analytical thinking with strong communication to bring clarity to complex data — you've found the right person.
