Who is Ayushi Sisodia?

Since my early studies I've been captivated by the stories numbers can tell. After earning a Bachelor’s degree in Finance, I discovered my passion for data and analytics and decided to pursue it wholeheartedly. My journey has taken me from building dashboards that inform HR strategy to forecasting sales trends and presenting insights to university leadership.

Today I'm deepening my expertise through an MBA in Business Analytics at Saint Peter’s University (GPA 3.8/4.0) after completing coursework in Human Resource Management at Thompson Rivers University and a B.Com in Finance from Mody University. Inspired by mentors and my own curiosity, I blend business acumen with technical skill to derive actionable insights.

If you're looking for someone who can use both their analytical and creative skills to work magic on your data, you've found the right person!
