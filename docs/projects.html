E-Commerce · Funnel Analysis · Conversion Optimisation

E-Commerce Customer Journey & Conversion Analysis

Problem: Map the full purchase funnel, identify where customers drop off, and surface the highest-impact opportunities to improve conversion yield.

Approach: Analysed 12,000+ customer interactions segmented by acquisition channel (Google, Email, Social, Direct, Referral), device type, cart behaviour, and peak activity windows. Assessed referral channel ROI and conversion rates at each funnel stage.

Output: Funnel visualisation identifying 3+ major drop-off points, recommendations for mobile checkout optimisation and targeted channel investment — projecting a 12–15% conversion yield improvement.
